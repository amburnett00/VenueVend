The Technology

The Machine

A modern, touchscreen vending kiosk purpose-built for nightlife — not retrofitted from a gas station.

🖥️
  • Full Touchscreen Display — Large interactive touchscreen makes browsing and buying fast and intuitive, even in a loud, dim bar environment.
  • Age-Verification Integrated — Built-in ID scanning and verification technology ensures every transaction is 21+ compliant.
  • Remote Inventory Monitoring — We track stock levels remotely and restock proactively — no staff involvement ever required.
  • Card & Contactless Payments — Accepts all major cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for frictionless checkout.
  • Compact Footprint — Designed to fit naturally in bar corners, hallways, or near restrooms without disrupting flow.
🔞

Strictly 21+ Environments Only. VenueVend machines are installed exclusively in licensed adult venues. Age verification is enforced at every transaction — we take compliance seriously.

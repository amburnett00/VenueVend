## Business Description
VenueVend provides smart, 21+ touchscreen vending machines for bars and nightlife venues in Manhattan, Lawrence, and the greater Kansas City area. We offer a zero-cost, passive income solution for venue owners, stocking high-demand items like premium vapes, nicotine pouches (ZYN, On!), and essential nightlife products. Our machines feature built-in age verification, remote inventory management, and accept all major payment methods, ensuring seamless operation and compliance. Partner with VenueVend to enhance your venue's offerings and generate effortless revenue without any staff involvement.

## Services Offered
*   **21+ Vending Machine Placement**: Free installation and placement of advanced touchscreen vending machines in eligible bars and nightlife venues.
*   **Full-Service Management**: Comprehensive management including stocking, maintenance, and technical support, ensuring machines are always operational and fully supplied.
*   **Age Verification Technology**: Integration of ID scanning and verification to ensure all transactions comply with 21+ age restrictions.
*   **Passive Income Generation**: Opportunity for venue owners to earn a percentage of sales without any operational overhead or staff involvement.
*   **Curated Product Selection**: Provision of high-demand products tailored for nightlife, including disposable vapes, nicotine pouches, phone chargers, and other essentials.
*   **Local Service Area**: Dedicated service and support for venues located in Manhattan, KS, Lawrence, KS, and the greater Kansas City area (KS & MO).
